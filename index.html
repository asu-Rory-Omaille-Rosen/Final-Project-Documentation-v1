Build your community organically with the help of the Ice Breaker app.

An application designed to make making friends easier. Through the Ice Breaker web app, you can build a short profile including your own personal icebreakers to invite new friends and acquaintances. If you're feeling outgoing, you can be the first to say hi.

Build a profile Prompts for conversations

Once you've built your profile, send local people in your community a "wave"

Sending other users a "wave" will share your short profile and Ice-breakers with others in your area. The application will suggest other local users in your area, send them a wave and it will share your profile with them. When you run into them locally, you will have an instant conversations starter built in.
